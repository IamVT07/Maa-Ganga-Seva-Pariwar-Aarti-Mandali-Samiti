## 🛕 About The Organization
**Maa Ganga Seva Pariwar Aarti Mandali Samiti** is a devoted cultural and social organization operating from Kanpur. Our core mission is the spiritual service of Mother Ganga and the upliftment of our local community. 

We actively organize grand daily/weekly Maha Aartis, massive river cleanliness drives (Swachhata Abhiyan), Bhandaras (community feasts), and spiritual gatherings at the local ghats to unite devotees for a noble cause.

## 🙏 Purpose of This Platform
This digital portal was created as an offering (Seva) to bring our devotee community closer. By making event participation and donations simple and transparent, this platform ensures that technology serves devotion, allowing more people to connect with Maa Ganga effortlessly.

## 🌟 Key Features for Devotees
* **📱 Seamless Booking via WhatsApp:** Devotees can book special Aartis, volunteer for cleanliness drives, or register for events directly through a simple WhatsApp integration. No complex forms, just direct communication.
* **🕊️ Secure & Transparent Donations:** A highly secure **Firebase** backend system handles all voluntary contributions and donations for the Samiti's welfare activities.
* **📧 Instant Digital Receipts:** Every donor immediately receives a verified donation receipt directly on their Email, ensuring complete transparency and trust.
* **🔔 Live Community Updates:** A digital notice board keeping the Pariwar updated about upcoming cultural programs, Swachhata Abhiyans, and Samiti meetings.
* **🖼️ Devotional Gallery:** A visual journey showcasing the divine Maha Aartis and the real-world social impact created by our volunteers.
